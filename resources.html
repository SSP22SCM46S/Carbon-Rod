How the algorithm works

Data from our device is encrypted and sent to an online datbase where it is then processed

By utilizing data from our sensors, geolocation, and object recognition from our cameras we are able to compute the optimal tree/shrub to plant where the Tree of Life is placed.

How does this device induce carbon sequestration

Biological (or terrestrial) sequestration involves the net removal of CO2 from the atmosphere by plants and micro-organisms and its storage in vegetative biomass and in soils.

Major factors to ensure maximum levels of biosequestration are soil health and type of vegitation planted. We accomplish this by incorporating these faactors into our algorithm.

What is our impact?

At scale, we estimate that our product has the potential to remove and sequester up to 10 gigatons of carbon dioxide per year.

This will take the the cooperation of all of us whether our device is used for an entire community, campus, or personal use.
